1. Executive Summary

The South African Union of Students (SAUS) is the official representative body for Student Representative Councils (SRCs) across all South African public universities. Founded in 2006 at Stellenbosch University, SAUS plays a critical role in higher education governance and student advocacy at a national level.

Following our initial engagement, the Lubelo Tech Solutions team conducted a thorough audit of SAUS's digital presence — including the website (www.saus.org.za), social media channels, and email systems. We identified significant design, content, and technical issues that undermined SAUS's credibility as a national institution.

Since that audit, Lubelo Tech Solutions has already delivered the core website upgrade. A fully custom, professionally designed SAUS website has been built from the ground up — replacing the broken WordPress site with a modern, authoritative, and fully responsive web presence. In addition, a custom Admin CMS has been developed, giving SAUS full control over its digital content without relying on any third-party platform.

Our core findings from the original audit:

  • The existing website had significant design, content, and technical issues that undermined credibility
  • Social media presence was fragmented and underutilised across all platforms
  • Email infrastructure needed to be properly configured and professionalised
  • There was enormous untapped potential to grow SAUS's visibility and influence through strategic digital engagement

This updated proposal reflects the work already completed, the improvements delivered compared to the old website, and the ongoing management investment required to keep SAUS's digital presence at its best.

3.2 Custom Admin CMS — A First for SAUS

The SAUS Admin CMS is a fully custom web application — built with Next.js, an Express.js API, and a PostgreSQL database. Designed and built specifically for SAUS's workflows and structure — not an off-the-shelf product.

4. Recommended Solutions

4.1 Email System Resolution

Lubelo Tech Solutions recommends migrating SAUS email to Google Workspace, which provides professional @saus.org.za addresses with 30GB storage, reliable delivery, proper SPF/DKIM/DMARC authentication, Google Drive, Google Calendar, and a full admin console. Microsoft 365 is an alternative if Outlook is preferred.

Immediate email fixes included in our management service:

  • Audit and correct all DNS records (MX, SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
  • Set up proper email accounts for all NEC members (president@saus.org.za, sg@saus.org.za, etc.)
  • Configure email forwarding, shared inboxes, and SAUS-branded signatures for all members
  • Implement email backup and archiving procedures
