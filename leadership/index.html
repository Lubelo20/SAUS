Office of the President

President — 9th National Executive Committee

Siyabonga Moses Nkambako

University of KwaZulu-Natal (UKZN)

President Nkambako leads the 9th NEC with a mandate to confront the suppression of student activism and advance transformative student governance across South African higher education institutions. Elected at the 9th National Elective Congress, he represents the aspirations of over one million students.
